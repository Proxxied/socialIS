Welcome to Solar Energy Negotiations

Excelling in providing universities the opportunity to easily
negotiate and purchase renewable energy solar panels from
our trusted manufacturing partners.


It's Easy and Free

Step 1: Create an account


Sign up to join other universities in becoming a
leading role model in our bid for clean and affordable
energy by 2030.

Step 2: Compare the plans


Compare the solar energy plans with our many trusted
manufacturing partners to see which type of renewable
energy is the right one for your university.

Step 3: Contact and Negotiate


Contact your manufacturer of choice and view their
response through your account's inbox.

Step 4: Accept and Purchase


Once both parties come into an agreement, press the
accept offer button and send payment to your desired
manufacturing partner.
